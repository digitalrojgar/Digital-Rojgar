Eligibility Criteria

Primary School Teacher (Code 1 to 4):

  • Passed MP TET (2020 or 2024)
  • 12th with 50% + 2-Year D.El.Ed OR
  • 12th with 45% + D.El.Ed (As per NCTE 2002) OR
  • 12th with 50% + 4-Year B.El.Ed OR
  • Graduation + 2-Year D.El.Ed
  • 5% relaxation for Reserved Categories

Primary School Teacher - Science (Code 5 to 10):

  • 50% marks in 12th (Science Stream)
  • 2-Year Diploma in Elementary/Special Education OR 4-Year B.El.Ed
  • Must be from recognized institute and qualified in MP TET
