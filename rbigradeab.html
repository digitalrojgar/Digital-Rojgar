Eligibility Criteria

  • Legal Officer: Bachelor's in Law with 50% marks (45% SC/ST/PwBD), 2 years post enrollment experience. Age 21-32 years.
  • Manager (Civil): Bachelor's in Civil Engineering with 60% (55% SC/ST) + 3 years relevant experience. Age 21-35 years.
  • Manager (Electrical): Bachelor's in Electrical/Electronics with 60% (55% SC/ST) + 3 years experience. Age 21-35 years.
  • Assistant Manager (Rajbhasha): PG Degree in Hindi/English + knowledge of translation/bilingual word processing. Age 21-30 years.
  • Assistant Manager (Protocol & Security): 10 years commissioned service (5 years for PwBD), valid ID, age 25-40 years (born between 2 July 1985 and 1 July 2000).
